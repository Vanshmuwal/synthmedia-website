Critical Issues
🔴 Critical
Value Proposition is Invisible

"We Build Brand Worlds" tells a cold visitor absolutely nothing. A visitor has ~5 seconds to understand your offer. Right now you fail that test.

Question a visitor asksDoes the site answer it?
What do you actually do?✗ No
Who is this for?✗ No
What result will I get?✗ No
Why you vs. alternatives?✗ No

Phrases like "high-fidelity visual narratives" and "synthesizes messaging" are internal jargon. Customers don't talk like this — and they won't stick around to decode it.

Fix → Rewrite the Hero
"Scroll-stopping AI videos for creators and brands — delivered in 7 days"

Lead with outcome, not aesthetic. Specificity > creativity in a headline.

🔴 Critical
No Real Social Proof

A $600/month commitment requires trust. Right now, there is nothing on the site that proves results for a real paying client.

What you haveWhat it signals to visitors
"Project Alpha, Neural Flow, Nexus One"Generic names — could be self-made demos
"Vansh and Prabodh (Agency Leads)"No photos, no credentials, no context
Zero testimonialsNo one has vouched for you publicly
No client logosNo recognizable names to borrow trust from
No performance metricsNo proof the work actually performs
Fix → Add Trust Layer
  • One testimonial with a real name, photo, and a specific number ("went from 2k to 40k views on our launch reel") would outperform all the current copy
  • Rename portfolio pieces to client/brand names, not internal codenames
  • Add view counts or engagement metrics under each portfolio video
🔴 Critical
CTAs Are Broken
Current CTAProblem
"Initialize Contact"Sounds like sci-fi, creates friction and confusion
"Start the Synthesis"Branded jargon, no value communicated
"Reply with package choice"Relies on email — massive drop-off point
"View Process"Soft, no action intent

The entire conversion flow ends at "reply to an email." No booking link, no intake form, no Calendly. Every step of friction after intent kills conversions.

Fix → Rebuild the Conversion Path
  • Replace "Initialize Contact" → "Get My First Video" or "Book a Free Discovery Call"
  • Embed a Calendly or Tally form — remove the email dependency entirely
  • Repeat the CTA after every major section, not just at the top and bottom
  • Add a low-commitment secondary CTA: "See sample work first →"

🟠 High
Pricing Is Hurting You

$100 for a "cinematic AI video" triggers doubt, not excitement. At that price point, the Van Westendorp "too cheap, questions quality" zone kicks in. Premium creative agencies don't price this low.

ProblemImpact
No "recommended" plan badgeVisitors don't know what to choose — they leave
No "best for" labels per planNo self-identification, kills confidence
100% upfront, no guarantee (Solo)High perceived risk, kills first purchase
No competitor price anchor$600 feels expensive without context
Round pricing used for low amountsPremium signal wasted at $100
Fix → Pricing Psychology
  • Add "Most Popular" badge to Creator Growth ($350)
  • Add "Best for: solo creators testing AI video" under each plan
  • Add a money-back or unlimited-revisions guarantee
  • Anchor with context: "Traditional agencies charge $3k–$15k per video"
  • Frame Solo as: "Your first video, risk-free"

Medium Priority
🟡 Medium
No Lead Capture for "Not Ready Yet" Visitors

~95% of visitors won't buy on their first visit. Right now, if they leave — they're gone forever. No retargeting, no email, no follow-up sequence.

Fix → Add One Email Capture
"Not ready yet? Get our free 7-point checklist for making AI videos that actually go viral."
  • Add a lead magnet (checklist, free brand audit, sample storyboard)
  • Install retargeting pixel (Meta/Google) so you can re-reach visitors with ads
  • Set up a 3-email nurture sequence for leads who don't buy immediately
🟡 Medium
No Analytics or Conversion Tracking

Without tracking, you're flying blind. You don't know where visitors drop off, what they click, or which traffic source converts.

Fix
  • Install Google Analytics 4 + set up a conversion event on "Initialize Contact" click
  • Install Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity (free) for heatmaps and session recordings
  • Install Meta Pixel for retargeting if you plan to run paid social
🟡 Medium
Missing Distribution Strategy

The website is the destination — but there's no clear engine driving traffic to it. The Rule of 7 applies: prospects need ~7 touchpoints before converting. One visit rarely closes.

Fix → Build a Flywheel
  • Short-form content: Post before/after video edits on Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts — your work IS the marketing
  • Case study posts: "How we took a creator from 3k to 50k views" performs well in creator/marketing communities
  • Cold outreach: DM mid-size creators (10k–100k followers) with a free sample edit as a hook
  • Community presence: Be active in creator economy communities where your buyers hang out
Psychology Gaps
Social Proof Missing

Bandwagon effect — no testimonials, client count, or logos to make others' trust contagious.

Anchoring Missing

Show traditional agency pricing ($3k–$15k) first so your prices feel like a deal by contrast.

Loss Aversion Missing

Frame around what they're losing without you — not what they'll gain. "Every day without a brand video is a day your competitor owns the feed."

Scarcity / Urgency Missing

"Only 3 client slots open this month" creates genuine urgency if true. Idle capacity is a missed conversion lever.

Reciprocity Missing

Give free value first — checklist, free audit, sample edit. Creates obligation to reciprocate.

Endowment Effect Missing

Offer a free sample edit so prospects "own" your work before paying. Dramatically reduces perceived risk.

Regret Aversion Missing

No money-back guarantee, no free trial, no "cancel anytime." Fear of regret is killing conversions.

Authority Weak

Mention tools (Veo, Sora, Kling) but no "featured in", no certifications, no authority signals beyond tool names.

Default Effect Missing

No recommended plan is pre-selected. The paradox of choice is killing decisions — guide visitors to one default.
